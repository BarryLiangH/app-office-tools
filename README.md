# app-office-tools

Looking for the tools for office daily jobs

## Overview

`app-office-tools` is a comprehensive office productivity system designed to streamline daily business operations. It helps professionals manage communications, schedules, translations, document analysis, and report generation all in one place.

## Features

### 1. **Communication Management**
- Monitor emails, WhatsApp, and WeChat messages in real-time
- Automatically detect and filter new messages
- Move spam and unwanted emails to the spam box with intelligent filtering

### 2. **Schedule & Deadline Reminders**
- Track your schedule and upcoming tasks
- Send alerts for document completion deadlines
- Notify relevant business departments about important milestones

### 3. **Translation Management**
- Perform translation jobs with configuration-based rules
- Define translation styles and guidelines in a configuration file
- Support multiple locales:
  - China
  - Singapore
  - Malaysia
  - Hong Kong
  - Taiwan
  - US
  - British English
  - And more...
- Maintain consistency with local UI standards (e.g., Adobe UI localization)

### 4. **Company Background Understanding**
- Perform web searches to gather information about your company
- Interactive Q&A with users to understand company background and context
- Build a knowledge base of company information for smarter document analysis
- Use company context to provide more relevant insights and recommendations

### 5. **Contract & Document Analysis**
- Automatically read and analyze contracts
- Identify key terms related to:
  - Company benefits
  - Tax implications
  - Import/export barriers and transportation regulations
  - Litigation risks
  - Cash flow impact

### 6. **Report Generation**
- Generate comprehensive analysis reports from uploaded documents
- Deep analysis of key points with detailed commentary
- Present findings from at least two opposing viewpoints for informed decision-making
- Alert company leadership to critical issues
- Support multiple output formats:
  - **PowerPoint (.pptx)** - For presentations and meetings
  - **PDF (.pdf)** - For sharing and archiving
  - **Excel (.xlsx)** - For data analysis and visualization

### 7. **Document Formatting & Styling**
- Apply text formatting:
  - **Bold**
  - *Italic*
  - <u>Underline</u>
  - ~~Strikethrough~~
- Strikethrough for marking content before deletion
- Bilingual support: English and Chinese
- Professional formatting for all generated documents
